Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about TaxiMeter Pro

How accurate is the GPS distance tracking?

GPS accuracy typically ranges from 3-15 meters depending on your device and location. The meter uses high-accuracy GPS mode and filters out GPS drift to provide the most accurate distance calculation possible. Urban areas with tall buildings may have slightly reduced accuracy.

Does the meter work offline?

GPS tracking works offline since it relies on satellite signals. However, the map tiles require an internet connection to load. The fare calculation and distance tracking will continue to work even without map visibility.

Can I use this for professional taxi services?

Yes! TaxiMeter Pro is designed for both personal and professional use. You can customize all fare rates to match your local regulations or company pricing. However, always verify with your local transportation authority for compliance requirements.

Why does the map show my location incorrectly?

Location accuracy depends on several factors: GPS signal strength, device capabilities, and surrounding environment. For best results, ensure location services are enabled and set to high accuracy mode. Moving outdoors or near windows can improve GPS reception.

Is my trip data stored or shared?

No. All calculations happen locally in your browser. We do not store, transmit, or share any trip data. Your location information never leaves your device. This ensures complete privacy for all your trips.

Understanding Taxi Fare Calculation

Taxi fare calculation typically involves three main components: a base fare that covers the initial pickup, a distance-based charge for the kilometers traveled, and a time-based charge for waiting or slow-moving traffic. Our taxi meter accurately combines all these factors to provide transparent and fair pricing.

The base fare represents the cost of entering the taxi, which varies by city and region. This covers the driver's time and fuel for the initial portion of the trip. The per-kilometer rate accounts for the actual distance traveled, measured precisely using GPS technology. For stops or slow traffic, the per-minute rate ensures fair compensation for the driver's time.

Using OpenStreetMap integration, our taxi meter provides accurate, real-time location tracking without relying on expensive proprietary mapping services. This open-source approach ensures reliability and privacy while maintaining professional-grade accuracy for all your fare calculation needs.
